92NY offers programs for children and families all year round at our Upper East Side location and beyond. Our roster of classes is designed to engage every child in the things they love. World-class teachers, all experts in their own fields and highly skilled at engaging young people, welcome children of all interests and abilities to classes and workshops. From prenatal classes and programs for new parents to pre-professional and portfolio prep programs for teens, 92NY will support and nurture your child’s journey of discovery every step of the way.

We want to make our programs work for all families in our community. Needs-based scholarships for 92NY programs are available on a case-by-case basis, and we welcome applications.
